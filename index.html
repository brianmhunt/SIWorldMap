The Sovereign Insolvency World Map

Visualizing the history of sovereign financial crises

Move your mouse cursor over (or click) a country to see the years they have defaulted.

Sources

The information in this map is stored in a Google Docs Spreadsheet. In particular, see the Sources tab of that spreadsheet for a list of sources used for this map.

This map was inspired by a similar map by Philip Wood Q.C.

This page was developed with jVectorMap on Google App Engine with Flask.
