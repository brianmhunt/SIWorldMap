Sovereign Insolvency World Map

A visual history of sovereign financial crises

Move your mouse cursor over, click or tap a country to see their history of default.

Sources

The information in this map is stored in a Google Docs Spreadsheet . In particular, see the Sources tab of that spreadsheet for a list of sources used for this map.

This map was inspired by a similar map by Philip Wood Q.C.

This page was developed (GitHub) with jVectorMap on Google App Engine with Flask.
